Coleen Scott, founder of Mama Coleen's bakery was born in San Mateo, California to the parents of Irish immigrants.
Mama Coleen in her favorite place, doing her favorite thing, baking.

On December 26, 1990, Mama Coleen's bakery opened in San Tan Valley, Arizona. It has been a staple in the community for over 30 years and still serves the East Valley with a wide variety of cakes, pies, cookies, and specialty items. Mama Coleen has been baking for over 50 years and doesn't seem to be slowing down any time soon!
